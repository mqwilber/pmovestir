# Metadata for the data cleaned_movement_data_08292024.csv

- animal_id: Unique animal ID
- x_: x location of animal in UTM (CRS: "+proj=utm +zone=16 +ellps=WGS84 +datum=WGS84 +units=m +no_defs")
- y_: y location of animal in UTM (CRS: "+proj=utm +zone=16 +ellps=WGS84 +datum=WGS84 +units=m +no_defs")
- t_: Time stamp of when fix was taken
- dop: Dilution of precision for GPS fix
- fix_status: Indicates the quality of the fix
- method_of_capture: How the animal was captured
- lat_capture: Latitude of where the animal was captured
- long_capture: Longitude of where the animal was captured
- age: Age of the animal
- sex: Sex of the animal
- duplicate_: Is this a duplicate point?
- fast_step_: Is this considered a fast step?
- fast_roundtrip_: Is this point associated with a fast round trip?
- longitude: Longitude of fix
- latitude: Latitude of fix
